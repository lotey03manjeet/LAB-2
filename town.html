This is my hometown.

My hometown is a small city. It is both traditional and modern because of having several festivals celebrated by the locals, and at the same time it is starting to be developed with increasing number of infrastructures and establishments.

My hometown, Rara Sahib is a small village located in the southern part of Ludhiana, Punjab.It is a small village but there are several tourist attractions.The town is both traditional and modern as it is starting to be developed just like other province having much more sophisticated infrastructure.
